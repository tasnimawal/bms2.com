Payments:


When paying for your chosen facility, be aware that the costs are mentioned once you click on your chosen space - the costs may or may not be similar, depending on the day you book your space.


The charges made for Beal's spaces are dependent on the clients requirements and are based upon an hourly rate. A refundable deposit of Â£250 is required. Please do keep in mind that we require each space to have Public Liability Insurance.


Before you pay for your deposit to secure your booking (via PayPal), a survey will need to be completed - you will find this along with the pricing and other details. The rest of the cost will need to be paid on the day of the appointment.

Discounts:


When paying for your chosen facility, the discounts are mentioned once you click on your chosen space - the discounts are the same with all of the facilities at BHS.


How it works is that after making 10 bookings at BHS, you then receive a discount from the website i.e., 5% off. With 20 bookings, it would then be 10% off and so on.
